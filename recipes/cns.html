Easy Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

This recipe is for easy chicken noodle soup. It is easy to follow and is great to make if you're not feeling well.

Ingredient List

  • 1 white or yellow onion
  • 2 russett potatoes
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 celery stalks
  • 1 package of egg noodles
  • cooking oil
  • better than boullin or any chicken boullin
  • shredded chees (optional)
  • chicken talk
  • rotistery chicken (costco)
  • chicken stock - 32oz

Steps

  1. Chop all vegtables: quarter onions and potatoes into bite size chunks. Chop carrots and celery at an angle. place all vegtables into a mixing bowl.
  2. Shred a decent amount of the rotistery chicken from costco. DO NOT THROW AWAY THE SKIN! EAT THE SKIN! Once shredded, place inside a container and set aside.
  3. Get a decent size pot and place on medium-high heat. Pour in cooking oil about 2 tbsp and dump vegtables mix. Cook until colored and tender.
  4. Pour in 32oz chicken stock and bring heat to high. Mix in boullin to taste. Once boiling let simmer for 10-15 min (until veggies are soft and a fork can go through the potatoes easily). Place on low.
  5. Pour in shredded chicken and mix. Let stand for 5 min and pour in egg noodle package. Once egg noodles are aldente. Turn head off and serve. I top with cheese but it is optional if you have no taste buds.
